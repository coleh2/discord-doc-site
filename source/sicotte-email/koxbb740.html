[Superintendent's Update] 2nd Dose Vaccination Clinic Registration

5/20/2021, 3:54:00 PM

Dear Needham Public Schools Families:

With gratitude to our parter, PelMeds,  the 2nd Dose COVID Vaccine Clinic for those children ages 12 and up who received their first COVID vaccine dose at Pollard Middle School on May 15th will be scheduled on Saturday, June 5th at the same location.

Please note: This clinic is only for those who received their first dose at Pollard on May 15th.

Please register here for a time slot between 9:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. Should you have any questions, please email:  info@pelmeds.com

Many thanks and stay healthy and safe,
