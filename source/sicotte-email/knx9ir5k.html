Returning from Vacation

4/25/2021, 10:24:11 AM

NHS Students and Families,
I hope your vacations have been great and relaxing! I also hope you've been able to stay safe while doing so. 

If you traveled, please be sure to send the student's PCR Test result to the Health Office before returning to school. Students will need a Negative PCR test result to return to in person class instruction.  We also ask that any students who received a PCR test after traveling, check in with the high school nurses for a quick temperature check-in, then they can return promptly to class. The NHS Health Office is open at 7:30 am. Monday-Friday. Any PCR Testing document(s) and completed Covid Vaccine documentation should be sent to the NHS_Nurses@needham.k12.ma.us. And if you are feeling at all sick or off, please stay home. 

Looking ahead, here are some reminders of key dates: 
  • Monday and Tuesday of this week are Gold Cohort days.
  • Thursday and Friday of this week are Blue Cohort days.
  • 9th and 12th grade students return in full on Monday the 3rd (and the 4th, 6th, and 7th). 10th and 11th will be home in synchronous classes. Wednesday will be remote for all students. 
  • 10th and 11th grade students return in full on Monday the 10th (and the 11th, 13th and 14th). 9th and 12th grade will be home. 10th grade students will take part in MCAS testing (ELA- Mon/Tue; Math- Thu/Fri). Wednesday will be remote for all students. 
  • All students return in full on Monday the 17th. 
  • In-person Wednesdays begin on Wednesday the 19th and continue through the remainder of the year. 
  • Remote Academy students will remain in that learning model through to the end of the year. 
  • Friday, May 28th is the last day for seniors (full day).
  • Friday, June 18th is the last day for underclass students (1/2 day).

Enjoy the last bit of the year!
