6/14/2021, 12:48:32 PM

Orders will be open until Tuesday, June 15th at 3 pm.




Hello NHS Community,

I am happy to announce that The Advocate has made it possible for NHS community members who missed the opportunity to order a 2021 yearbook another chance to do so. This, however, comes with a bit of a cost to offset the reprinting expenses. The new cost of the yearbook will be $95 a book. 

To secure a 2021 yearbook, visit MySchoolBucks to pre-order a yearbook. This is the only way to secure a yearbook. Orders will be open until Tuesday, June 15th at 3 pm. Unfortunately, there will be no exceptions. 

Please note the following:

1. We will not be accepting cash or checks at this time. 
2. There is no waitlist - everyone who has already inquired must go through the same process in pre-ordering on MySchoolBucks
3. This is first-come, first-serve and there will be no exceptions. 
4. The shipment and distribution of yearbooks will most likely come in late July. 

If you are a 2021 Senior, please make sure to add an email address that is not your NHS email so we are able to contact you when the yearbooks come in. You will find this option on MySchoolBucks. 

If you are in need of financial assistance please contact Nicole Burnor directly at nicole_burnor@needham.k12.ma.us - we have The Advocate Scholarships available for those who qualify. 


All the best,

Ms. Burnor
