Next Week at NHS

1/29/2021, 2:34:42 PM

NHS Community,
Needham High School will be open for in-person learning next week. Our Blue and Green cohorts students will be able to return to the hybrid model with us starting Monday. Our Gold and Remote Academy cohort students will be at home as scheduled. 

The spike in cases we had last weekend subsided dramatically, and we have not had a positive case reported since Tuesday. That means we do not have the contact tracing challenges or impact that we did a week ago, nor do we have a greater likelihood of exposure than we did a week ago. The building is now as safe as it has been for the 17 weeks of our hybrid model before we hit this week. 

There is one thing I want to highlight: 
If students want to be in-person at NHS, they need to follow our protocols in the building AND need to make safe choices outside of the building. There can be no gathering on the weekends and evenings in groups or without masks, if we want to be in person. 


Enjoy your weekends, everyone! And be safe!
A. Sicotte
