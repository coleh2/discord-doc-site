One Day 2021 is this Wednesday! - April 28th


This year marks our 5th annual One Day. The purpose of One Day is to build a stronger sense of community by providing opportunity for students, faculty, and staff to learn more about the wide range of identities that make up our NHS family. We hope One Day allows you to learn more about others and connect in a way not offered through our daily schedule. 


Our One School One Book last summer was Symptoms of Being Human by Jeff Garvin. The book is centered around Riley Cavanaugh, a gender fluid high school student navigating the challenges of relationships and being a teenager. Our theme for the day is: “I belong here: Free to Be Who I Am.” We recognize that in order to truly build a vibrant community we must first know ourselves so we can support one another. We are a community of almost 2,000 individuals that come together everyday to learn from one another. There is a lot that we know about each other...and there’s a lot that we don’t know about each other, by collectively participating in One Day we hope to change the latter.


Throughout the day we would like you to:

  • get to know one another better;

  • create a school community that is inclusive, welcoming, and safe for all students, faculty, and staff;

  • understand that, although we come from different backgrounds and experiences, we all want the same things: to have friends, to be part of a community, and to work and play in an environment that is built on mutual respect for one another.


As you experience the day, be open to learning something new about yourself, learning about other members of your community, and allowing for others to learn something about you.


To make the most out of the experience for everybody, keep the following guidelines in mind…

  1. Share from the heart

  2. Listen from the heart

  3. Respect participants and facilitators

  4. Respect the confidentiality of all participants

  5. Try on something new…

  6. And most of all….Have fun!


Help us to make our NHS One Day a success!

One Day 2021 Programing Description:


This year’s One Day will be a completely virtual webinar event consisting of two main segments. The event will begin with a collaboration between NHS Alumni and our Culture & Climate Committee providing foundational information and vocabulary on gender identity and guidance on how we can create a welcoming inclusive community. In addition to hearing from our guests, segments will include quotes from our One School, One Book:  Symptoms of Being Human and artwork from current NHS students.


The second half of One Day will be a panel discussion facilitated by Greater Boston PFLAG. The panelists will share their experiences and provide some insights and strategies on how we all can be allies for our LGBTQ friends and family.
