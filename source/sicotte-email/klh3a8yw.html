Course Selection Update and Resources

2/22/2021, 4:25:53 PM

Dear Students and Families, 


I hope you are healthy and doing well. 


I am reaching out with an update about the Course Selection Process and Timeline. This week teachers will finalize student course recommendations for the 2021-2022 school year. Beginning next Monday, March 1st, students will have the opportunity to review their level recommendations in PowerSchool and make course and elective requests for next year. During the following week, March 8th through March 12th, parents/guardians will have the opportunity to review and discuss recommended courses and other course requests with their students. All overrides are due by Friday, March 12th.
