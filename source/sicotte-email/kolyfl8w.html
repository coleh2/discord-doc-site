[Superintendent's Update] Vaccine Clinic for Needham Youth Update

5/12/2021, 5:08:02 PM

Dear NPS Families:

Our incredible partner, PelMeds Pharmacy, has decided to provide additional slots for this Saturday's vaccine clinic in the Pollard gyms for Needham and Needham METCO students ages 12 and up.

If you are interested, please register for the May 15, 2021 Pollard Vaccine Clinic.  Should you have any additional questions, please email PelMeds directly: info@pelmeds.com

Consent forms and other information are available when you register.  Registration for the second clinic at Needham High School on May 22nd will be made available soon.

Thanks and stay healthy,
