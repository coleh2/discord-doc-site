Student Ambassador Information Sessions

3/22/2021, 12:57:52 PM

NHS is looking for rising juniors and seniors to volunteer as "Ambassadors" during the 2021-2022 school year. The Ambassador Program works to create and maintain a sense of welcome and community, as well as offer continual guidance and support for all students new to Needham High School! We are looking for students who are interested in the following: 
  • Welcoming students who are new to Needham: Grade 9, Transfers, ELL
  • Organizing and leading tours 
  • Attending two “orientation” days in August
  • Welcoming students on the 1st day of school 
  • The first week, acting as one-on-one “guides” - limited numbers
  • Implementing activities for X-block the first two weeks of school
  • Attending a mandatory training session 
  • Earning community service credit

Optional:
  • Being a “self-selected leader” who would participate in meetings during the school year to create, revise, and update materials and procedures, as well as creating surveys and maintain the website 
  • Attending an “Ambassador leaders planning day” 
  • Earning additional community service credit

If you are interested in becoming an Ambassador, please come to one of two virtual information sessions during X-block:
  • Monday, March 29th at 1:45pm
  • Wednesday, April 7th at 1:00pm
Click here to join the Zoom on 3/29 or 4/7!

If you are interested but cannot attend, please email Mrs. Kannon or Mrs. Brinkmann.
