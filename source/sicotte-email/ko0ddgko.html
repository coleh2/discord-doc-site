One Day is Tomorrow!

4/27/2021, 2:35:21 PM



One Day is tomorrow- Wednesday 4/28/2021!!!



One Day will take place on Wednesday April 28 and during periods B & C of Remote Wednesday Schedule.  Students with a period B class should report to their class Zoom promptly at 8:35.  Your period B teacher will take attendance and provide you with a link to our YouTube live webinar link.   


If you do not have a period B class or if you are unable to access your period B zoom for some reason you can use the link below to access the live stream: https://youtu.be/m_30cJiN9u8


Part of One Day will be a panel discussion facilitated by Greater Boston PFLAG. The panelists will share their experiences and provide some insights and strategies on how we all can be allies for our LGBTQ friends and family.  You may ask a question to our guest panelists using this Google Form. We will do our best to answer as many questions as possible.


If anything comes up during the event that you would like some support around please follow the link below to speak with someone from guidance. This zoom will be open until 11:00AM: https://needham-k12-ma-us.zoom.us/j/8...
