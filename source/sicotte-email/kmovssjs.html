NHS Students and Staff,
I want to add my voice to the celebration of the historic swearing in of Kim Janey as Boston's Acting Mayor. There have been nearly 50 people who have served as mayor of Boston since the first was elected in 1822. Now, for the first time, the city will be led by a woman, and for the first time the city will be led by a Black leader. We've seen history get made today.

Politics aside, it is a huge moment for the city. Having representation and models in all areas of life for all people matters, and this is a great step for the city and surrounding area. Since many of our students and staff live in Boston, I especially wanted to highlight today's swearing in. 
In celebration,
